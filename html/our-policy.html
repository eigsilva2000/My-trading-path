Community Guidelines

Be Respectful to Others

  • Treat all members with courtesy and respect.
  • Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of harassment.
  • Foster a positive and supportive environment.

Only Post Trading-Related Content

  • Ensure that all posts, blogs, podcasts, and YouTube channels are directly related to trading.
  • Avoid off-topic discussions or unrelated content.

No SPAM

  • Do not engage in spamming activities, including unsolicited promotional content.
  • Refrain from repetitive or irrelevant posting.

No Discrimination

  • Do not discriminate against others based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or any other factors.
  • Foster a community where everyone feels welcome.

Respect Privacy

  • Avoid sharing personal information about yourself or others without explicit consent.
  • Respect the confidentiality of private discussions and trades.

No Financial Advice

  • Do not provide or seek financial advice within the community.
  • Encourage members to consult with financial professionals for personalized advice.

Legal and Ethical Standards

  • Adhere to all relevant legal and ethical standards related to trading.
  • Do not engage in activities that violate laws or regulations.

Cite Sources

  • When sharing information or analyses, provide proper citations and attribute sources.
  • Avoid plagiarism and respect intellectual property rights.
